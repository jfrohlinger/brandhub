How it works:
The brandhub operating system.

Four connected phases — one platform. No spreadsheets. No gaps. No guessing.

01  /  PLAN
Plan
Programs, items & order windows configured
selectbrandhub.com/admin/programs/179/items
Program Admin
  • ✦Programs — brand team gets its own program with goals, items & channel rules
  • ✦Item Admin — POS product portfolio with SKUs, MOQs, lead times, variants & supplier assignments
  • ✦Order Windows — time-gated purchasing periods with territory & channel controls
  • ✦Demand Planning — distributor-level quantity forecasting before orders open
02  /  ORDER
Order
Sales teams place orders from a brand-approved product portfolio
selectbrandhub.com/order-sets/36228
Pre-Order Grid
  • ✦Branded storefront — sales reps see only their territory's approved items
  • ✦Pre-Order & On Demand — planned programs vs. in-season pull
  • ✦Multi-distributor forms — allocate to CA Wholesale, Johnson Bros in one screen
  • ✦Budget Impact shown before checkout + Approve / Deny in-portal
03  /  EXECUTE
Execute
Competitive sourcing & one-off procurement
selectbrandhub.com/rfqs/213
Quote Request
  • ✦Quote Requests — brief a spec, set qty tiers & in-market date; suppliers respond in-platform
  • ✦Open vendor platform — Acme, Moxie, ITC Cutting Edge & more compete for every item
  • ✦brandhub demandIQ helps you order exactly what you need, where you need it
  • ✦Awarded quotes link directly to POs — no re-entry, no re-routing
04  /  MEASURE
Measure
Every order, every dollar — full visibility
selectbrandhub.com/order-sets/36228/overview
Order Overview
  • ✦Order Activity — geographic map shows spend by market, territory & distributor
  • ✦Real-time performance tracking dashboards
  • ✦Linked invoicing — invoices tied to each PO & shipment for full cost transparency
  • ✦Program Reporting — production timelines, freight, on-time delivery per program
